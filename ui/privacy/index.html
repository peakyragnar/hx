What We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address: Used for account creation/sign-in (e.g., magic links), receipts, and important service notices.

Payment Information

Handled by Stripe. When you subscribe, your payment details are collected and processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store full credit card numbers. Stripe may share limited billing information (such as the last four digits or payment status) so we can manage your subscription. See Stripe’s privacy notice for more details.

Minimal Technical Data

We may process IP address, user agent, device/browser information, and timestamps in server logs, fraud checks, and rate-limiting systems. These are used to secure the service (abuse prevention, diagnostics) and are not sold.

Optional Analytics

If we enable privacy-respecting analytics in the future, we will disclose the provider here and update this policy. We do not run cross-site targeted advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: run evaluations, display results, enforce plan limits, and maintain your account/session.
  • Customer support: respond to requests, fix issues, and notify you of important changes.
  • Quality, safety, and improvement: debugging, abuse prevention, model quality evaluation, and product research (e.g., aggregate analysis of anonymized results).
  • Billing: manage subscriptions and invoices via Stripe.
  • Legal compliance: comply with applicable laws, court orders, and enforce our terms.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

How We Share Information

We share information with processors/service providers working on our behalf under contract, including:

  • Email provider (to send magic links and service emails).
  • Hosting & database providers (to run the app and store data).
  • AI model providers (to process your queries).

We may also share information for legal and safety purposes, or in business transfers such as a merger or acquisition. Processors must use data only to provide services to us and protect it appropriately.

International Transfers

We may process data in the United States and other countries where our providers operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for cross-border transfers.

Data Retention

  • Backups: may persist for a bounded period before rolling off.

You can request deletion at any time (see “Your rights & choices”).

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information (encrypted transport, restricted access, least-privilege credentials, periodic reviews). No system is perfectly secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique email account and keep your devices safe.
